HOW IT WORKS
1
Save your contacts — up to 3 people. Phone numbers stored only on your device.
2
Select your situation — Panic, Medical, Accident, Unsafe, Disaster, or Emergency.
3
Tap BEACON — GPS captured instantly. Siren fires. SMS sent to all contacts with your coordinates and a Google Maps link.
4
Repeats every 5 minutes — you will be prompted to re-send your location automatically until you tap BEACON again to cancel.
5
Tap again to cancel — sends a final "ALL CLEAR" message to your contacts.
